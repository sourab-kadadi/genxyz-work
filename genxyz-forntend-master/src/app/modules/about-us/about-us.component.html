About Us

GenXYZ.work  is a platform focused on helping companies to discover remote working talent around the world* and support them in hiring and managing the payroll, benefits, etc., of the onboarded employees through our employer of record services model.

On the other side,  GenXYZ.work empowers candidates to find suitable remote jobs helping them remain flexible with the work locations.

The large corporates always had an added advantage to scale up quickly by hiring the best talents across the world on account of the sheer availability of resources(time and money). We are here to break the barriers and bring equitable advantage to the small and mid-scale businesses and startups to hire the best talent at lower expenses all over the world*.
